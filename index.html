SIEGE SPACE X

Interplanetary Strategy Card Game

Project Description

Siege Space is a card game that has its own history with different characters from different planets and systems. Those races then fight against each other to protect their systems or to destroy others.

Functional Requirements

The game is played between 2 or more people. Each player gets an equal amount of in-game money, which they can use to buy their fighters (characters). Each character has their own skills and abilities. There are different types of characters like support characters, who help to earn more money during the game, fighters, who fight and destroy enemy fighters, and more. The player who ends up destroying other players' fighting squads and survives wins.

Technical Requirements

JavaScript, GitHub Repository, and a hosting platform will be used to deploy our product's website.
